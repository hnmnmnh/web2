BTS

Kim Seok-jin (Hangul: 김석진), better known by his stage name Jin (진) is a vocalist and the official visual of BTS. He is the oldest member, born on December 4th, 1992 (age 25). Jin made his debut on June 13, 2013, as a member of BTS on Mnet's M! Countdown with the track "No More Dream" from their debut single album 2 Cool 4 Skool. Together with the members of BTS, he went on to win a series of awards and performed at several events such as the Mnet Asian Music Awards (MAMA).
