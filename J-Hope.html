BTS

Jung Ho-seok (Hangul: 정호석), better known as J-Hope, is the main dancer and rapper of South-Korean Kpop group BTS(AKA; Bangtan Sonyeondan/ Beyond The Scene). J-Hope made his debut on June 13, 2013 as a member of BTS, a group formed by BigHit Entertainment, with their debut single album 2 Cool 4 Skool.
